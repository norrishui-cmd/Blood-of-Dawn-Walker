Quick answer

Coen has three confirmed skill trees: a human tree, a vampire tree, and a shared tree. This structure supports specialized day or night builds while preserving abilities that matter in both forms.

What the latest official material confirms

Human and vampire play differ in traversal, combat, and available powers. Separate trees let progression reflect that duality instead of treating transformation as a purely cosmetic change.

The shared tree provides a bridge between forms. Official commentary also says players can focus heavily on one form, although doing so means missing a large portion of the other form’s content and options.
