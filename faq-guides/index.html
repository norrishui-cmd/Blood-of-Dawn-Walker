Latest Game Data Answers

Browse verified answers about newly documented characters, key objects, special systems, and current demo access.

Character

Bakir in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Bakir is a vrakhiri lieutenant serving Brencis. Official launch material describes a past shaped by the eastern steppes and credits Dai Tabuchi as his voice actor, with MMA champion Jan Błachowicz contributing motion capture.

Character

Father Florin in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Father Florin is a church figure whose surviving writings connect him to the crisis in Vale Sangora. Official lore says he tried to track villagers and recorded events as fear and disappearances spread.

Item

Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor

The Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor Set is a preorder and special-edition early unlock. Bandai Namco also states that the set can be obtained later through normal game progression, so it is not permanently exclusive.

Item

Legendary Sword in Svartrau Crypts

An official gameplay sequence follows a clue about a legendary sword believed to be hidden in the Svartrau crypts. The wording is deliberately uncertain: the sword’s exact location, name, stats, and acquisition steps have not been confirmed.

Gameplay

Activation Charges Explained

Activation Charges are a combat resource used to activate abilities. Official footage shows Coen building charges through combat actions and spending them on skills, making resource timing part of the directional melee system.

Gameplay

Burning Blood Ability

Burning Blood is a demonstrated ability that applies damage over time. It lets Coen pressure a target after the initial activation while continuing to move, defend, or set up another attack.

Gameplay

Artery Attack Ability

Artery Attack is a demonstrated combat ability capable of decapitating an enemy. Official footage uses it as a decisive finishing action after Coen creates an opening in melee combat.

Demo status

The Blood of Dawnwalker Demo

No public demo, open beta, or downloadable player test has been officially announced as of July 19, 2026. Media hands-on sessions and footage captured from development builds are not public demos.
