Human And Vampire Forms

A practical guide framework for planning daytime human actions, nighttime vampire power, and the tradeoffs between both sides of Coen.

Needs Hands-On TestingCore Gameplay Topic

Why The Two-Side Structure Matters

The game's premise makes Coen's human and vampire sides central to route planning. The key guide question is not simply which form is stronger; it is when each side is useful, risky, or restricted.

Testing Checklist

  • Which actions are safest during the day?
  • Which powers become available or stronger at night?
  • Can NPC reactions, stealth, or quest access change by form?
  • Does power use affect story consequences or endings?
  • How does the 30-day timeline interact with form-specific routes?

Guide Structure After Launch

Each quest, boss, and build page should include a human/vampire note when form choice affects the route. This creates practical internal links instead of isolated lore pages.
