Confirmed Edition Contents

Standard Edition
Full game. Eligible preorders include the Sangoran Wayfarer's Armor Set as an early unlock.
Day 1 Edition
Full game, SteelBook, world map, and the preorder armor early unlock at participating retailers.
Eclipse Edition
Full game, digital world compendium, digital comic book, digital soundtrack, and the preorder armor early unlock where applicable.
Collector's Edition
Full game, SteelBook, 33 × 40 cm world map, 23 cm PureArts Coen figure, 60-page hardcover world compendium, and the Eclipse digital extras.

The Sangoran Wayfarer's Armor Set is an early unlock, not permanently preorder-exclusive; official listings say it can be obtained later in the game.

What Is Not Included

No story DLC or early-access period is listed in the announced Eclipse or Collector's Edition contents. Prices, physical stock, and participating-retailer bonuses can differ by country, so verify the exact local listing before purchase.

Safe Buying Advice

Until all storefront pages are live and consistent, players should verify the edition name, platform, refund policy, and region before purchase. Performance-sensitive PC players should also wait for final system requirements and early technical impressions.

Buyer Questions To Answer

  • Which edition includes only the base game?
  • Are digital extras cosmetic, soundtrack/artbook items, or future gameplay content?
  • Is any bonus exclusive to preorder, or can it be earned later?
  • Does the physical edition differ by region or retailer?
  • Does a premium edition include early access, and if so, on which platforms?
