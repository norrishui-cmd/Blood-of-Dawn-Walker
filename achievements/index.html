FAQ

When should achievement guides become detailed?
After the official achievement or trophy list is visible and unlock conditions can be tested.
What should be tracked for achievements?
Track hidden achievements, missables, difficulty requirements, ending requirements, collectible requirements, and bugged unlocks.
Should a platinum guide be indexed before confirmation?
It can exist as a status page, but exact route advice should wait for trophy list and completion testing.
