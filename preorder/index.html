FAQ

Is preorder information final before launch?
Preorder information can change by region and store, so the page should separate confirmed store listings from unverified claims.
What should players check before preordering?
Players should check platform, edition contents, refund rules, preload status, language support, and whether any early access claim is official.
Can preorder bonuses affect walkthrough planning?
Only if bonuses include gameplay items and are officially confirmed. Until then, the guide should avoid build or route claims based on preorder content.
