Spoiler Policy

Quest pages should begin with a spoiler-light summary, then separate major story consequences under clear warnings. This lets players solve progression problems without accidentally reading ending details.

Launch Priority

The first walkthroughs should cover the opening route, early side quests, quests with time windows, and any mission that can fail or lock rewards. These are more useful than a full but shallow list.
