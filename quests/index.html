Spoiler Policy

Quest pages should begin with a spoiler-light summary, then separate major story consequences under clear warnings. This lets players solve progression problems without accidentally reading ending details.

Launch Priority

The first walkthroughs should cover the opening route, early side quests, quests with time windows, and any mission that can fail or lock rewards. These are more useful than a full but shallow list.

FAQ

What makes a quest page valuable?
A valuable quest page tracks trigger, location, time window, choices, rewards, missables, related characters, and consequence links.
Should quest walkthroughs be published before launch?
Only framework pages should exist before launch. Exact objectives and outcomes require hands-on verification.
How should spoiler-sensitive quests be handled?
Use spoiler-light summaries at the top and hide ending or character-fate details deeper on the page with clear warnings.
