Ambrus in The Blood of Dawnwalker Explained

Meet Ambrus, the youngest disclosed vrakhiri: his human origins, ambition, personality, voice actor, and place in Brencis’s vampire faction.

Verified July 18, 2026Official-source facts

Quick Answer

Ambrus is the youngest vampire in Brencis’s disclosed vrakhiri group. Born into hardship and humiliation, he sought vampiric power deliberately and now uses charm, deception, and ambition to prove himself.

Confirmed lore and gameplay are separated from details that still require release-build testing.

Why Did Ambrus Become a Vampire?

Unlike a victim transformed without a goal, Ambrus sought vampiric power to rise beyond his human circumstances. His mortal life was marked by pain and humiliation. Official lore says there is no line he will not cross, suggesting that his desire for status and revenge matters as much as hunger. The exact sire, date, and rite of his transformation remain unknown.

Why Is He Called the Youngest?

The profile calls Ambrus the youngest vampire of the pack, meaning the youngest within the disclosed group around Brencis, not necessarily the youngest vampire anywhere in the setting. Xanthe reaches back to Ancient Greece and Brencis to Roman antiquity, so Ambrus has less seniority and feels pressure to demonstrate his usefulness to older peers.

Appearance and Personality

Polished armor and carefully styled hair make him look aristocratic despite humble origins. The contrast is intentional: he has remade his image to embody the status once denied to him. He is described as charming, deceptive, and dangerous, with speech serving his own purpose. Players should therefore treat apparent cooperation as character information, not proof of loyalty.

Voice Actor and Gameplay Status

Lorne Macnaughton voices Ambrus. Official sources establish him as a central vrakhiri character but have not disclosed his quest chain, location, boss mechanics, romance status, or possible endings. Searches for an Ambrus fight or choice outcome currently exceed confirmed evidence and should be revisited when gameplay or review coverage supplies concrete answers.

How to Use This Pre-Release Guide

This entry records what Rebel Wolves and Bandai Namco have actually disclosed before launch. It does not convert folklore from another game into Dawnwalker mechanics, and it does not treat a trailer edit as proof of a fixed quest route. Exact map coordinates, dialogue outcomes, weaknesses, rewards, and missable conditions will be added only when the release build or a new primary source makes them verifiable. That distinction keeps the page useful now without forcing players to unlearn invented details later.
