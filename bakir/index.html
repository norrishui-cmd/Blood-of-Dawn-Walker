Quick answer

Bakir is a vrakhiri lieutenant serving Brencis. Official launch material describes a past shaped by the eastern steppes and credits Dai Tabuchi as his voice actor, with MMA champion Jan Błachowicz contributing motion capture.

What the latest official material confirms

Bakir belongs to the vrakhiri, the vampire elite ruling Vale Sangora under Brencis. That places him inside the central occupying power rather than among Coen’s initial allies.

The performance credits are unusually specific: Dai Tabuchi provides the voice and Jan Błachowicz performed motion capture. Those facts help identify the character across trailers and promotional footage.
