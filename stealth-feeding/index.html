Why This Topic Matters

Vampire RPG players search for stealth and feeding rules because those mechanics often affect combat readiness, NPC reactions, morality, or story outcomes. This page prepares a responsible tracker without inventing rules early.

Testing Questions

  • Does feeding restore health, power, or another resource?
  • Can witnesses trigger alarms, quest failure, or reputation changes?
  • Are there safe feeding routes or non-lethal alternatives?
  • Does feeding interact with endings or character relationships?
  • Are stealth tools different between human and vampire states?

Guide Value After Launch

This page should become a route and consequence guide, linking to vampire powers, choices, quests, and endings whenever feeding decisions matter.
