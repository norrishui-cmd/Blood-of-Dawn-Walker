Burning Blood Ability

Quick answer

Burning Blood is a demonstrated ability that applies damage over time. It lets Coen pressure a target after the initial activation while continuing to move, defend, or set up another attack.

What the latest official material confirms

The effect was shown as part of the ability system powered by Activation Charges. That makes it a tactical option within combat rather than a passive story power.

Official material has not published duration, damage scaling, resistance interactions, stacking rules, or the precise skill-tree node needed to unlock it. Those values can change during balancing.

Evidence boundary

Burning Blood should not be confused with Blood Hunger or Boiling Blood, which relate to Coen’s vampiric condition and nighttime pressure. It is a named combat effect demonstrated against enemies.
