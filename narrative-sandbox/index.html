What Narrative Sandbox Means For Guides

A narrative sandbox needs a different guide structure from a linear walkthrough. Players need help deciding what to do next, what can wait, and which choices may lock content.

Content Elements Needed

  • Non-spoiler route priority for the first playthrough.
  • Quest time cost and urgency labels.
  • Point-of-no-return warnings.
  • Character and ending consequence links.
  • Alternative route notes for replay value.

Why This Is Not Thin Content

This page is a structural guide: it explains how players should use the site's quest, choice, ending, and 30-day pages together. It becomes stronger as tested route data is added.
