A spoiler-controlled structure for tracking ending branches, major choices, and replay requirements after launch.

Ending Data TBASpoilers Controlled

Spoiler Policy

Ending pages should be useful without immediately revealing story outcomes. The first section should list non-spoiler requirements, while detailed endings should sit below clear spoiler warnings.

What To Track

  • Number of confirmed endings once known.
  • Major quest choices that affect branches.
  • Character or faction outcomes.
  • Point-of-no-return warnings.
  • Replay steps needed to see alternate endings.

Why This Page Matters

Ending searches are high-intent after launch. A careful page can attract players who want planning help while preserving trust through spoiler controls.
