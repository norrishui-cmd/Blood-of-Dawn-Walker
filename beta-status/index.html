Quick answer

Official footage has been described as running on a current PC beta version, but no public beta has been announced. “Beta build” refers to the development state of the captured game, not an invitation for players to download it.

What the latest official material confirms

Studios commonly label internal or presentation builds by development milestone. The wording is useful evidence that footage comes from an in-progress PC version, but it does not establish an open or closed community test.

No official beta registration page, eligibility rules, dates, platform list, NDA terms, or key distribution method has been published. Third-party pages promising access should be treated cautiously.
