Release Date, Preorder, and Editions

When is The Blood of Dawnwalker coming out?
September 3, 2026. The date is confirmed for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. An exact global unlock timetable has not yet been published, so local store timing may differ.
Is The Blood of Dawnwalker delayed?
No delay from the announced September 3, 2026 date has been confirmed as of July 16. Check the release date tracker if a storefront shows a different regional date.
Can you preorder The Blood of Dawnwalker?
Yes. Digital and selected physical preorders are open. Availability, price, and delivery date vary by platform, country, and retailer.
What is the preorder bonus?
The preorder bonus is an early unlock of the Sangoran Wayfarer's Armor Set. Official listings state that the set can also be earned later in the game, so it is not permanently locked behind preorder.
What editions are available?
Bandai Namco lists Standard, Day 1, Eclipse, and Collector's editions. The Standard edition contains the full game. Day 1 adds physical items in participating markets; Eclipse adds a digital compendium, comic, and soundtrack; Collector's adds a SteelBook, map, hardcover compendium, and 23 cm Coen figure.
Does the Eclipse Edition include story DLC or early access?
No story DLC or early-access period is listed in the announced Eclipse Edition contents. Its extras are the digital world compendium, comic book, and soundtrack, plus the preorder armor early unlock where applicable.
Is there a physical edition?
Yes. Day 1 and Collector's physical editions are announced, while exact stock and contents can vary by region and retailer. Verify the product format before ordering.
Is there a demo or beta?
No public demo, open beta, or playable trial has been announced as of July 16, 2026.

The 30-Day Time Limit Explained

Does The Blood of Dawnwalker really have a 30-day time limit?
Yes, Coen has 30 in-game days and nights to try to save his family. It is a narrative resource system, not a real-time countdown running every second you play.
Does exploring use up time?
No. Developers say you can explore, read, and idle without the clock continuously advancing. Time is spent by narratively meaningful actions.
Which actions advance time?
Confirmed examples include some quests, certain dialogue choices, and learning particular abilities. The game shows the cost before a significant action consumes time.
Do all quests consume time?
No official source says every quest consumes time. Treat each displayed time cost separately instead of assuming all main and side content uses the same amount.
What happens when the 30 days run out?
It is not an automatic game over. Developers say the world and story continue with consequences. The precise family and ending outcomes are intentionally unrevealed.
Can you finish most of the game before time runs out?
The creative director says players should be able to complete a majority of the game before the deadline. Doing absolutely everything in one run has not been promised.
Can you go straight to Brencis?
After reaching a certain story point, the game allows a direct attempt against Brencis. Going early means facing a much harder challenge without the allies, knowledge, and power gained from optional stories.
Can time be paused, reset, or reversed?
Exploration does not drain the clock, but a manual time rewind system has not been announced. Save and reload behavior around spent time needs hands-on verification.

Story, Character Creation, Romance, and Choices

Who do you play as?
You play as Coen, a young man from Vale Sangora who becomes a Dawnwalker while trying to rescue his captured family.
Can you create a custom character or choose Coen's gender?
No custom protagonist or gender selection has been announced. Coen is a predefined male character, while dialogue and actions shape his personality, relationships, and path.
Is Coen a full vampire?
No. He is human by day and vampire by night, sharing the strengths and weaknesses of both states. This duality changes combat, traversal, magic, and quest solutions.
Who is Brencis?
Brencis is the vampire lord ruling Vale Sangora and holding Coen's family. Player actions can undermine his power and provoke changes such as stronger patrols or restrictions.
Who is Lunka?
Lunka is Coen's younger sister. Her illness and encounter with Brencis are central to the prologue and the family's struggle.
Does The Blood of Dawnwalker have romance?
Yes. Developers have confirmed romance and the ability to build relationships, but romanceable characters, exclusivity rules, and requirements remain unrevealed.
Are there companions?
Coen can form alliances and befriend characters who may help his cause. A conventional always-following party or companion-control system has not been fully detailed.
Do choices matter?
Yes. Both action and inaction can change quests, NPC survival, alliances, the balance of power, and story outcomes. Important characters can die without ending the playthrough.
Does The Blood of Dawnwalker have multiple endings?
Developers have confirmed multiple endings and multiple solutions to stories, but the number and unlock requirements have not been revealed.
Where is the game set?
The story takes place in Vale Sangora, a fictional region in the Carpathian Mountains during a dark reimagining of 14th-century Europe amid war and the Black Death.

Open World, Combat, Vampire Powers, and Playtime

Is The Blood of Dawnwalker open world?
Yes. Vale Sangora is described as a handcrafted open world with settlements, ruins, caves, mines, swamps, hidden paths, and narrative-driven activities.
Can you switch between day and night?
The game is built around choosing day or night approaches, with different abilities, encounters, and solutions. The exact rules for changing time on demand still need final hands-on verification.
How is human Coen different from vampire Coen?
By day, Coen relies on swordplay and dark hex magic. By night, he gains vampire mobility, claws, bites, supernatural strength, and other powers, while blood hunger becomes a major risk.
Can Coen use magic?
Yes. Human Coen can discover and upgrade hexes carved into his skin. One revealed spell, Compel Soul, can communicate with the dead and open new quest information or branches.
How does combat work?
Combat mixes directional melee attacks, blocks, parries, dodges, magic, vampire abilities, and activation charges used for powerful skills. An optional Omni-Block system is designed to make defense more accessible.
Is The Blood of Dawnwalker a Soulslike?
It is officially marketed as an action RPG, not a Soulslike. It has timing and enemy-pattern mechanics, but its structure centers on narrative choice, open-world exploration, and two character forms.
Can Coen feed on animals instead of people?
Yes. Developers have said Coen can feed on animals including deer, bears, and wolves, offering alternatives to harming human NPCs.
What happens if the blood hunger meter gets too low?
Severe hunger can overwhelm Coen. A demonstrated consequence replaces dialogue choices with an unavoidable option to give in and feed, which can kill an NPC and alter the story.
How long is The Blood of Dawnwalker?
Internal developer playthroughs have reportedly taken about 55 to 70 hours. Your time may differ substantially depending on exploration, chosen quests, difficulty, and replay goals.
Is there New Game Plus?
New Game Plus has not been confirmed as of July 16, 2026.

Will it run at 60 FPS on PS5 or Xbox Series X?
Final console graphics modes and performance targets have not been fully confirmed. Avoid treating PC footage labeled 4K/60 as proof of console performance.
Is Steam Deck support confirmed?

Monsters, Vampires, and Locations

Who are the vrakhiri?
The vrakhiri are the ancient vampires ruling Vale Sangora. Brencis leads the disclosed group, which includes Xanthe, Ambrus, and Bakir. See the vrakhiri guide.
Who is Xanthe?
Xanthe is the oldest disclosed vrakhiri, formerly a priestess in Ancient Greece. She creates Blood Guards and uses blood-control powers.
What are Blood Guards?
They are human servants whose bodies and minds are bound to the vrakhiri through dark blood rites. They are stronger than normal human enemies.
Are Blood Guards vampires?
They are humans tainted and transformed by vampire blood, not members of the ancient vrakhiri group.
What are kobolds?
Kobolds are small creatures that control Vale Sangora's mines and attack distracted prey in groups from tunnels and shadows.
What are the ancient undead?
They are former warriors and guardians trapped between life and death, still protecting someone or something within old ruins.
What is a psoglav?
A psoglav is a canine corrupted after a vrakhiri fang becomes lodged in its body, creating a blood-driven predator.
Are there werewolves in Dawnwalker?
No werewolf or lycanthrope has been confirmed. The studio instead names murohni, kobolds, uriashi, likhos, psoglavs, and other creatures.
What is Svartrau?
Svartrau is the silver-rich capital of Vale Sangora. Cellars, caves, crypts, and timeless ruins form a maze under the city.
Where is Howling Keep?
Local stories place the disappearing mansion deep in the Tantari Woods. Exact access conditions and its lady's identity remain unrevealed.
What happened at Shrike's Crag?
Skender Dragosti massacred surviving peasant rebels at the old quarry. Locals consider the blood-soaked site cursed.
Is Vale Sangora a real place?
No. It is a fictional Carpathian valley inspired by Central and Eastern European landscapes, history, folklore, and cultures.

Combat, Movement, and Progression

Can Coen ride a horse?
No. Rebel Wolves explicitly said Coen does not have a horse.
Does The Blood of Dawnwalker have fast travel?
Yes. The developers confirmed fast travel through the world map, although unlock rules and individual nodes are still unknown.
Can Coen use bows or crossbows?
No. Coen learned sword fighting from his father and the developers say he cannot use bows or crossbows.
Can Coen fight with his fists?
Yes. The gameplay recap confirms punches and blocks as an unarmed combat option.
How many skill trees does Coen have?
Three have been shown: a human tree, a vampire tree, and a shared tree used by both forms.
What are Activation Charges?
They are combat resources built through successful attacks, blocks, and parries, then spent on Active Abilities.
What does Burning Blood do?
Burning Blood is a revealed hex that applies damage over time. Final values, upgrades, and resistances are not yet published.
What does Omni-Block change?
It reduces the need to manually match every incoming block direction. It is an optional defensive aid, not automatic parrying or invulnerability.
Can you focus only on human or vampire Coen?
Yes, but the developers warn that relying on only one form means missing a large portion of the game’s abilities and content.

Routes, Quests, and Time Planning

Can Xanthe be defeated early?
Yes. The official demonstration ends in defeat, but Rebel Wolves states that the early Xanthe battle is winnable for a sufficiently skilled player.
How many Blood Guards protect Xanthe in the gameplay demo?
Three Blood Guards must be defeated in the demonstrated cathedral route before Xanthe descends to fight Coen.
How can you tell whether an activity consumes time?
Time-spending activities display an hourglass icon and show how far they will advance the world before completion.
Will a timed-looking event continue while you explore?
The developers use a burning-house activity to explain that the scene can remain available while the player explores because real-world minutes do not advance narrative time.
Are quests divided into main quests and side quests?
Rebel Wolves says the narrative sandbox does not use a meaningful traditional division; players decide which stories matter to their route.
Is the legendary sword in Svartrau mandatory?
The gameplay presentation follows clues to a sword in Svartrau’s crypts, but the studio has not said that every playthrough must complete that objective.
Can important NPCs die without ending the game?
Yes. Choices, combat, and critical hunger can kill characters while the wider playthrough continues with consequences.

Story, Characters, and World

Will the first Dawnwalker game have a complete ending?
Yes. Rebel Wolves says Coen and Brencis’s conflict will receive a self-contained conclusion without leaving the central plot unresolved.
Does the modern-day teaser mean the first game has time travel?
No. The developers explicitly say the first game remains in the 14th century; the modern setting teases future standalone installments.
Will future Dawnwalker games use other eras and countries?
That is the stated plan. Future installments are intended to explore different eras, locations, cultures, stories, and characters.

He is Vale Sangora’s former knyaz, remembered for crushing a peasant rebellion at Shrike’s Crag before disappearing during a manhunt.
Who is Father Florin?
He is a local priest whose diary records villagers’ habits, fears, and subtle changes as Vale Sangora approaches a new dawn.
Can relationships unlock new paths?
Yes. Official material says trust and connections can reveal hidden truths and open paths that shape Coen’s destiny.
Who are Ambrus and Bakir?
Ambrus is the youngest disclosed vrakhiri and a deceptive social climber; Bakir is a powerful, bloodthirsty lieutenant of Brencis.
