The Blood of Dawnwalker Combat Guide

A combat guide structure for launch-week testing: survivability, damage windows, ability use, and boss preparation.

Combat Data TBABuild Testing Needed

Combat Systems To Test First

  • Dodge timing, invulnerability windows, and stamina or resource costs.
  • Block, parry, interrupt, stagger, and recovery behavior.
  • How weapons or abilities scale with upgrades.
  • Healing availability and punishment windows.
  • Enemy aggression changes by time of day or region.

Launch-Week Combat Advice

Until exact numbers are known, players should avoid overcommitting rare resources. A good early route is to invest in survival, learn enemy timing, then specialize once respec rules and scaling are confirmed.

Boss Preparation

Each boss entry should connect to recommended builds, useful abilities, safe punish windows, and phase changes. This creates useful internal links between combat, bosses, and builds.
