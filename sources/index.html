Sources And Editorial Policy

How this guide separates confirmed facts, hands-on testing, speculation, and post-launch updates.

Trust PageUpdated July 3, 2026

Source Policy

Confirmed facts should come from official sources, storefronts, publisher announcements, developer posts, or clearly attributed reputable coverage. Unreleased gameplay details should be labeled as TBA, needs testing, or template-only.

Testing Policy

After launch, walkthroughs, builds, boss weaknesses, map routes, endings, and achievements should be updated only after hands-on verification or clearly cited evidence.

FAQ

Why does the site need a source policy?
A pre-release guide site must show which facts are official, which need testing, and which should not be published yet.
What sources are strongest?
Official websites, publisher pages, storefront listings, developer posts, patch notes, and hands-on tested evidence are strongest.
How should corrections be handled?
Correct changed claims quickly, update the last verified date, and link affected pages through updates and sources.
