Quick answer

Yes. The developers confirm that Coen can fight with bare fists. Unarmed combat is therefore a supported fallback or play option, although a complete fist-focused build has not been detailed.

What the latest official material confirms

The confirmation matters if Coen is disarmed or a player wants to experiment without a conventional weapon. It does not establish that fists match the damage or reach of swords.

No official source has published unarmed skills, finishers, scaling attributes, block behavior, equipment bonuses, or whether every encounter can be completed efficiently without weapons.

Evidence boundary

Players should separate “possible” from “optimal.” The launch build will need testing before claiming that a full unarmed run, achievement, or dedicated skill path exists.
