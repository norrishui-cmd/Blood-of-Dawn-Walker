Quick answer

Peregrini Aurorae is an inscription visible on a sword in official key art and is explained as “Dawn’s Pilgrim.” It is a confirmed visual and thematic detail, not a confirmed weapon name, item drop, or proof of a quest reward.

What the latest official material confirms

The phrase reinforces the Dawnwalker identity and the series’ imagery of transition between day and night. Its presence on key art makes it relevant lore, but promotional composition does not establish inventory behavior.

Official sources have not linked this inscription to the legendary sword sought in the Svartrau crypts. Treating the two as the same item would be an unsupported inference.
