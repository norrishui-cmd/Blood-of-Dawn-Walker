Quick answer

No public demo, open beta, or downloadable player test has been officially announced as of July 19, 2026. Media hands-on sessions and footage captured from development builds are not public demos.

What the latest official material confirms

Xbox Wire published a hands-on preview on July 7, 2026, which shows that selected media received access. That does not provide a download, signup, or general public play opportunity.

Bandai Namco has also described footage captured from a current PC beta build. In this context “beta” identifies the development build used for the presentation; it is not an announcement of a public beta program.

Evidence boundary

Players should rely on the official game page and publisher news for any future test. Avoid third-party “demo downloads,” key generators, and signup pages unless an official account links to them.
