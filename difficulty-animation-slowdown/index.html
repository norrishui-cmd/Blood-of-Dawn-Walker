Combat Animation Slowdown

Official gameplay commentary says easier difficulty settings can slow combat animations. The option gives players more time to interpret attack directions and respond within the melee system.

Officially sourcedUnconfirmed details labeled

Quick answer

Official gameplay commentary says easier difficulty settings can slow combat animations. The option gives players more time to interpret attack directions and respond within the melee system.

What the latest official material confirms

Directional combat asks the player to read incoming strikes and choose a defensive response. Slowing animation speed changes the reaction window without removing the core visual information.

The published explanation does not provide named presets, exact percentages, per-enemy behavior, or a complete accessibility menu. Those details may change before release.

Evidence boundary

This feature should not be described as a universal slow-motion toggle until the final settings are visible. The confirmed point is that easier modes can make animations slower to improve readability.
