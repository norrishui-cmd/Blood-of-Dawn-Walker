Latest Game Entity Database

New source-checked profiles for characters, items, special gameplay, and demo status.

Character

Bakir in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Bakir is a vrakhiri lieutenant serving Brencis. Official launch material describes a past shaped by the eastern steppes and credits Dai Tabuchi as his voice actor, with MMA champion Jan Błachowicz contributing motion capture.

Item

Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor

The Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor Set is a preorder and special-edition early unlock. Bandai Namco also states that the set can be obtained later through normal game progression, so it is not permanently exclusive.

Gameplay

Activation Charges Explained

Activation Charges are a combat resource used to activate abilities. Official footage shows Coen building charges through combat actions and spending them on skills, making resource timing part of the directional melee system.

Gameplay

Three Skill Trees

Coen has three confirmed skill trees: a human tree, a vampire tree, and a shared tree. This structure supports specialized day or night builds while preserving abilities that matter in both forms.

Demo status

The Blood of Dawnwalker Demo

No public demo, open beta, or downloadable player test has been officially announced as of July 19, 2026. Media hands-on sessions and footage captured from development builds are not public demos.

Time System

Exploration does not drain the clock

Meaningful actions spend time, while ordinary exploration and reading do not run down the 30-day limit.

Dual Gameplay

Human by day, vampire by night

Human Coen uses swordplay and hex magic; vampire Coen gains claws, supernatural movement, and blood hunger.

PC Specs

Updated requirements are lower

The current baseline starts at 16 GB RAM, a GTX 1060 or RX 580, and 60 GB of SSD storage.
