Launch Week

Player-Focused Content Checklist

This is the first place a pre-release guide site can create value: show players what to track before launch, then replace the placeholders with tested guidance as soon as the game is playable.

Content Priority

  1. Day 0: Release date, platforms, PC requirements, language support, editions.
  2. Day 1: Beginner guide, difficulty choice, first three hours, common blockers.
  3. Week 1: Bosses, region maps, builds, major choices, ending requirements.
  4. Long term: Collectibles, achievements, DLC, patch database, community submissions.
