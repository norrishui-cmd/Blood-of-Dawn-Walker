Quick answer

Zdislav is identified in official bestiary material as the alderman of Laslea. His diary records growing danger around the settlement and helps establish how ordinary authorities interpret the creatures threatening Vale Sangora.

What the latest official material confirms

As alderman, Zdislav provides a civilian perspective distinct from Brencis and the vrakhiri. His notes connect local governance, rumor, and eyewitness fear.

The diary appears alongside illustrated creature material, making it useful context for enemies and regional lore. It is not confirmation that Zdislav is a recruitable ally or a quest giver.
