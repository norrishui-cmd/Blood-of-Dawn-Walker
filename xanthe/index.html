Xanthe in The Blood of Dawnwalker Explained

Xanthe is the oldest vrakhiri, an ancient Greek priestess connected to Blood Guards, blood magic, and a cathedral encounter in Svartrau.

Verified July 18, 2026Official-source facts

Quick Answer

Xanthe is the oldest disclosed vrakhiri, once an ancient Greek priestess whose original name is lost. She creates Blood Guards, hears voices beyond the ordinary world, and uses blood-control powers in combat.

Confirmed lore and gameplay are separated from details that still require release-build testing.

How Old Is Xanthe?

Official material calls her older than seems possible and traces her human life to Ancient Greece. It does not provide a birth year or exact vampire age. Time has damaged both body and mind, yet her apparent instability is not simple madness: she understands a deeper aspect of the world and is frightened by what she perceives.

What Is Her Role Among the Vrakhiri?

Xanthe is one of the central vampires serving within Brencis’s ruling group. Her age gives her a perspective unlike younger Ambrus or the politically ambitious Brencis. The bestiary also names her as the person who strips Blood Guards of their humanity, connecting her occult knowledge to the faction’s transformed human enforcers.

How Does the Xanthe Fight Work?

In the official Svartrau demonstration, Coen enters the cathedral, defeats three Blood Guards, and then fights Xanthe. She attacks with blood whips from multiple directions and uses a protective vampire ability. When her stamina is exhausted, Coen gains an opening, but she can counterattack and attempt to restore blood. Final phases, difficulty changes, and rewards are unknown.

Is Xanthe Always a Boss?

The presentation shows several ways to handle the preceding conversation but chooses violence for the demo. That is not proof that every route requires killing her. Dawnwalker emphasizes choices and alternative approaches, so the encounter context may change. Until release, it is safest to call Xanthe a confirmed combat opponent in the demonstrated route, not an unavoidable boss.

How to Use This Pre-Release Guide

This entry records what Rebel Wolves and Bandai Namco have actually disclosed before launch. It does not convert folklore from another game into Dawnwalker mechanics, and it does not treat a trailer edit as proof of a fixed quest route. Exact map coordinates, dialogue outcomes, weaknesses, rewards, and missable conditions will be added only when the release build or a new primary source makes them verifiable. That distinction keeps the page useful now without forcing players to unlearn invented details later.
