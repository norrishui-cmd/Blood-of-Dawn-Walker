Quick answer

Activation Charges are a combat resource used to activate abilities. Official footage shows Coen building charges through combat actions and spending them on skills, making resource timing part of the directional melee system.

What the latest official material confirms

Charges sit alongside attack direction, defense, stamina-like pacing, and ability selection. The player cannot treat every special move as freely available; actions that build charges create windows for stronger effects.

The exact number earned by each attack, maximum capacity, decay behavior, and skill costs may depend on build or balancing. Preview footage should not be converted into a permanent numerical table.

Evidence boundary

A useful launch guide will test charge generation by weapon and skill, but the confirmed pre-release takeaway is strategic: ordinary combat feeds the resource that enables activated abilities.
