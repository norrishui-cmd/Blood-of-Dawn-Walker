# Blood of Dawnwalker German and Spanish SEO Expansion

Date: 2026-07-18

## Delivered

- 12 indexable German URLs under `/de/`
- 12 indexable Spanish URLs under `/es/`
- Localized landing pages, metadata, navigation, direct answers, source sections, and internal links
- Reciprocal `hreflang` for English, German, Spanish, and `x-default`
- Separate `sitemap-en.xml`, `sitemap-de.xml`, and `sitemap-es.xml`
- Combined sitemap plus multilingual sitemap index

## Quality Boundary

The 171 English placeholder routes remain `noindex, follow` and were not translated. German and Spanish URLs are published only where the English site already has a concrete, source-checked answer. This prevents multilingual thin-page multiplication.

## Deployment

Deploy the full package and submit `https://bloodofdawnwalker.cc/sitemap-index.xml` in Google Search Console. The existing domain property can discover all three language sitemaps. Monitor country, query, and page performance separately for Germany, Spain, and Spanish-speaking markets.
