Vale Sangora: World and Locations Guide

A verified guide to Vale Sangora, including Svartrau, Howling Keep, Shrike’s Crag, Tantari Woods, mines, ruins, swamps, and mountain routes.

Verified July 18, 2026Official-source facts

Quick Answer

Vale Sangora is a fictional valley in the Carpathian Mountains, shaped by Central and Eastern European landscapes and cultures. Its confirmed landmarks include the capital Svartrau, Howling Keep, Shrike’s Crag, ancient ruins, mines, swamps, forests, and mountain paths.

Confirmed lore and gameplay are separated from details that still require release-build testing.

Svartrau and the Silver Trade

Svartrau is the valley’s capital and became wealthy through silver. Below the city, thieves use cellars, caves, crypts, and timeless ruins to escape the guard, while unknown creatures occupy the deeper dark. The cathedral and rooftops also appear in official gameplay, where vampire traversal opens routes that human Coen cannot approach in the same way.

Howling Keep and Tantari Woods

The Tantari Woods contain the legend of Howling Keep, a mansion that appears above the trees on some days and vanishes on others. Its mourning lady is said to reveal the gates according to a traveler’s intent. The location is official, but her identity, the entry conditions, and the consequences inside remain deliberately hidden.

Shrike’s Crag and the Valley’s History

Shrike’s Crag is an old quarry where Knyaz Skender Dragosti massacred peasant rebels. Its curse predates Brencis’s public takeover, showing that Vale Sangora’s violence did not begin with the vrakhiri. Modern visitors reportedly disappear. The source does not reveal whether the danger is supernatural, human, or connected to another disclosed creature.

Biomes and Exploration

The broader world includes primeval woodland, dangerous swamps, deep caves, remote mountain paths, active settlements, and ruins of lost civilizations. Exploration does not continuously drain the 30-day clock, but access and solutions can depend on form, powers, quests, and time of day. Exact map boundaries, region count, and fast-travel network are not yet confirmed.

How to Use This Pre-Release Guide

This entry records what Rebel Wolves and Bandai Namco have actually disclosed before launch. It does not convert folklore from another game into Dawnwalker mechanics, and it does not treat a trailer edit as proof of a fixed quest route. Exact map coordinates, dialogue outcomes, weaknesses, rewards, and missable conditions will be added only when the release build or a new primary source makes them verifiable. That distinction keeps the page useful now without forcing players to unlearn invented details later.
