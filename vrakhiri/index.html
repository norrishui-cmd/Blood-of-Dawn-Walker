The Vrakhiri Vampires in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Who the vrakhiri are, why Brencis rules Vale Sangora, and how Xanthe, Ambrus, and Bakir differ within Dawnwalker’s vampire faction.

Verified July 18, 2026Official-source facts

Quick Answer

The vrakhiri are the ancient vampire rulers of Vale Sangora. Brencis leads the group, with Xanthe, Ambrus, and Bakir among his disclosed lieutenants. They emerged from the shadows after Skender Dragosti disappeared in 1347.

Confirmed lore and gameplay are separated from details that still require release-build testing.

Who Belongs to the Vrakhiri?

Official profiles identify Brencis as leader, Xanthe as the oldest vampire, Ambrus as the youngest of the disclosed pack, and Bakir as one of Brencis’s most powerful lieutenants. Their ages, cultures, and motives deliberately span centuries. “Vrakhiri” therefore names the ruling vampire group, not every blood-tainted servant, murohni, psoglav, or supernatural enemy in the valley.

How Did They Take Vale Sangora?

The takeover occurs in 1347 after the old knyaz, Skender Dragosti, goes missing during a manhunt. Brencis steps forward with his lieutenants and the vampires become the valley’s public masters. Official marketing leaves room for inhabitants to perceive them as saviors amid plague and disorder, but their blood rites, prisoners, and coercive rule make the cost of that protection central to Coen’s conflict.

Are All Vrakhiri the Same?

No. Brencis is a Roman-born ruler driven by unrealized ambition. Xanthe is an ancient Greek priestess who perceives forces beyond ordinary understanding. Ambrus is charming, status-conscious, and eager to prove himself after a painful human life. Bakir is a steppe-raiding warmonger who wants his violence witnessed. These profiles signal different conversations and confrontations rather than interchangeable vampire bosses.

Vrakhiri, Blood Guards, and Murohni

Blood Guards are humans altered and oath-bound to serve the vrakhiri. Murohni are described by the developers as failed vampires. Psoglavs are corrupted canines created through a vrakhiri fang. Keeping these categories separate improves both lore accuracy and search relevance: the vrakhiri are the ancient masters at the center of the political faction.

How to Use This Pre-Release Guide

This entry records what Rebel Wolves and Bandai Namco have actually disclosed before launch. It does not convert folklore from another game into Dawnwalker mechanics, and it does not treat a trailer edit as proof of a fixed quest route. Exact map coordinates, dialogue outcomes, weaknesses, rewards, and missable conditions will be added only when the release build or a new primary source makes them verifiable. That distinction keeps the page useful now without forcing players to unlearn invented details later.
