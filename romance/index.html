The Blood of Dawnwalker Romance Guide

Romance and relationship-building are confirmed; specific partners and route requirements remain unrevealed.

Romance ConfirmedCharacters TBA

Current Status

Creative director Mateusz Tomaszkiewicz has confirmed that players can build relationships and pursue romance. The studio has not yet identified every romanceable character, whether routes are exclusive, or how much in-game time relationship scenes consume.

Relationship Fields To Track

  • Character name and first meeting point.
  • Whether the relationship is romantic, friendly, faction-based, or quest-only.
  • Required dialogue choices and missed opportunities.
  • Whether choices affect endings or achievements.
  • Major spoiler warning level.

Quality Rule

No romance route should be published until confirmed by official information, in-game testing, or clearly cited reliable coverage. This protects players from fake route claims.
