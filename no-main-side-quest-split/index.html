No Traditional Main and Side Quest Split

The developers say the game does not divide content into traditional main and side quests. Leads belong to the same narrative sandbox, and their importance comes from player goals, timing, and consequences.

Quick answer

The developers say the game does not divide content into traditional main and side quests. Leads belong to the same narrative sandbox, and their importance comes from player goals, timing, and consequences.

What the latest official material confirms

This design discourages treating optional-looking stories as disposable checklist content. A local lead can reveal information, change relationships, or affect the larger struggle against Brencis.

The 30-day structure means choosing one lead can leave another unresolved. The lack of labels does not mean every activity has equal scope or reward; it means the interface does not define a single privileged quest lane.

Evidence boundary

Exact journal categories, failure states, and completion rules must be checked in the release version. The confirmed principle is narrative parity, not a promise that all quests are mandatory.
