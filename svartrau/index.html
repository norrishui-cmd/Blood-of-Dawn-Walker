Location Guide

Svartrau: Capital of Vale Sangora in Dawnwalker

Explore what is known about Svartrau, its silver wealth, underground ruins, cathedral, thieves, city guard, and vampire presence.

Verified July 18, 2026Official-source facts

Quick Answer

Svartrau is the silver-rich capital of Vale Sangora. Beneath its streets lies a network of cellars, caves, and timeless ruins; above ground, its cathedral and rooftops feature prominently in the official gameplay demonstration.

Confirmed lore and gameplay are separated from details that still require release-build testing.

Why Is Svartrau Rich?

Svartrau’s growth and wealth came from the silver trade. That prosperity attracted thieves while supporting a determined city guard. The official location bulletin presents the capital as more than a medieval backdrop: its economy explains conflict on the surface, while passages below offer routes for fugitives and threats that predate the current regime. Exact shops, districts, and services remain unpublished.

What Is Under the City?

A maze of musty cellars, damp caves, and ancient ruins runs beneath Svartrau. Thieves use the network to evade the city guard, but rats and bats are only the obvious inhabitants; an unidentified growl warns that deeper corridors contain larger dangers. The gameplay reveal also follows a search for a legendary sword associated with crypts in the capital.

Svartrau Cathedral and Xanthe

The official gameplay route takes vampire Coen across rooftops and into the cathedral using supernatural traversal. Xanthe waits inside with three Blood Guards. The sequence confirms that the city supports vertical night routes and a major vrakhiri encounter. It does not prove that fighting Xanthe is mandatory, fixed to one time, or limited to the exact approach shown.

Can You Explore Svartrau by Day and Night?

The demonstration shows both forms in the capital and emphasizes different navigation and abilities. Human Coen can investigate and use carved-rune hexes, while vampire Coen can use Shadowstep, Clawride, and Planeshift for rooftops and walls. Quest availability and NPC routines may differ, but a complete day/night activity list needs release verification.

How to Use This Pre-Release Guide

This entry records what Rebel Wolves and Bandai Namco have actually disclosed before launch. It does not convert folklore from another game into Dawnwalker mechanics, and it does not treat a trailer edit as proof of a fixed quest route. Exact map coordinates, dialogue outcomes, weaknesses, rewards, and missable conditions will be added only when the release build or a new primary source makes them verifiable. That distinction keeps the page useful now without forcing players to unlearn invented details later.
