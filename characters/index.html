Character Guide Goals

A useful character page should help players understand who matters without spoiling late-game consequences. It should separate basic introductions from quest outcomes and ending-related details.

Fields To Track

  • Name, role, first location, and related questline.
  • Faction or allegiance if confirmed.
  • Dialogue choices that affect rewards or story outcomes.
  • Whether the character is tied to endings, bosses, or missable content.
  • Spoiler warning level for each section.

Internal Link Strategy

Character entries should link to quests, endings, and world-map locations. This creates a stronger guide structure than isolated biography pages.

FAQ

What should character pages include?
Character pages should include role, related quests, choice impact, relationship status, spoiler warnings, and links to endings or outcomes once verified.
Which characters should be prioritized?
Prioritize Coen, Lunka, Brencis, companions or major NPCs, and characters tied to endings or missable quests.
How should unconfirmed character outcomes be handled?
Mark them as TBA or needs testing and avoid presenting rumors as route facts.

Confirmed Character Database

These profiles use official lore and launch material, with unknown quest outcomes clearly separated.

Character

Bakir in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Bakir is a vrakhiri lieutenant serving Brencis. Official launch material describes a past shaped by the eastern steppes and credits Dai Tabuchi as his voice actor, with MMA champion Jan Błachowicz contributing motion capture.

Character

Father Florin in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Father Florin is a church figure whose surviving writings connect him to the crisis in Vale Sangora. Official lore says he tried to track villagers and recorded events as fear and disappearances spread.

Character

Vicho in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Vicho is described through official Vale Sangora material as a collector and hoarder of unusual curiosities. His account of an ivory figurine that appears to bleed is one of the valley’s published supernatural mysteries.

Character

Zdislav in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Zdislav is identified in official bestiary material as the alderman of Laslea. His diary records growing danger around the settlement and helps establish how ordinary authorities interpret the creatures threatening Vale Sangora.

Character

Skender Dragosti in The Blood of Dawnwalker

Skender Dragosti was the former knyaz of Vale Sangora. Official lore associates his rule with a massacre of peasants and says he later disappeared, leaving his fate unresolved in the material published so far.
