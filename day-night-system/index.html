The Blood of Dawnwalker Day And Night System

The day and night structure is one of the most important systems to track. This page defines what should be tested at launch.

Needs TestingCore Guide Topic

Why Time Matters

For an open-world vampire RPG, time can shape power availability, NPC routines, enemy pressure, and quest access. A useful guide must explain not just what changes, but how players should plan around those changes.

Testing Checklist

  • Which quests can start only during a specific time period?
  • Do enemies gain or lose abilities at night?
  • Are traversal routes safer during the day or night?
  • Do vampire powers have restrictions, costs, or story consequences?
  • Can the player freely wait, rest, or skip time?

Guide Format After Launch

Each quest page should include a time-window field. Each boss page should include whether time of day affects the arena, phase behavior, or available preparation routes.
