Are There Bows or Crossbows in The Blood of Dawnwalker?

No. Official gameplay commentary states that there are no bows or crossbows. The combat focus is close-range directional melee, abilities, vampiric powers, and other tools rather than traditional archery.

Quick answer

No. Official gameplay commentary states that there are no bows or crossbows. The combat focus is close-range directional melee, abilities, vampiric powers, and other tools rather than traditional archery.

What the latest official material confirms

This answer helps set build expectations: players should not plan an archer class around longbows, shortbows, bolts, or arrow crafting.

The statement does not mean every ability is melee-only. Supernatural powers and other combat effects can still influence targets at range, but they are not a conventional bow weapon category.

Evidence boundary

No weapon list is complete before release, so this page avoids extending the claim beyond what was said. It confirms the absence of bows and crossbows, not the absence of every ranged interaction.
