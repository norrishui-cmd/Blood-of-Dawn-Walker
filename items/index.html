Items & Key Objects

Equipment availability, quest clues, and lore objects—without invented stats or drop locations.

Current verified entries

Pre-release material confirms some equipment and story objects but does not yet expose a complete item database. Each entry below answers what is known and labels what still needs release-version testing.

Item

Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor

The Sangoran Wayfarer’s Armor Set is a preorder and special-edition early unlock. Bandai Namco also states that the set can be obtained later through normal game progression, so it is not permanently exclusive.

Item

Legendary Sword in Svartrau Crypts

An official gameplay sequence follows a clue about a legendary sword believed to be hidden in the Svartrau crypts. The wording is deliberately uncertain: the sword’s exact location, name, stats, and acquisition steps have not been confirmed.

Item

Bleeding Ivory Figurine

The ivory figurine appears in official Vale Sangora lore through Vicho’s account and is described as bleeding. It is a confirmed story object, but it has not been confirmed as a collectible inventory item or quest reward.

Item

Peregrini Aurorae Sword Inscription

Peregrini Aurorae is an inscription visible on a sword in official key art and is explained as “Dawn’s Pilgrim.” It is a confirmed visual and thematic detail, not a confirmed weapon name, item drop, or proof of a quest reward.

Database standard

Indexable item pages require a real named object and a useful confirmed answer. Exact stats, rarity, upgrade materials, vendor prices, and acquisition routes will only be published when supported by the final game or official documentation.
