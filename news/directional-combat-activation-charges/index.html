Directional Combat and Activation Charges Explained

The gameplay reveal shows fast sword exchanges that build resources for active abilities.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

Coen attacks, blocks, and parries from different directions. Successful combat actions build Activation Charges that can be spent on abilities such as Burning Blood and Artery Attack.

Why This Update Matters

The system rewards reading openings and switching quickly between offense and defense rather than becoming locked into long animations.
