September 3 Release Confirmed for PC, PS5, and Xbox Series

Bandai Namco confirms the release date and three launch platform families.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

The Blood of Dawnwalker will launch September 3, 2026 on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. No PS4, Xbox One, or Nintendo version is listed.
