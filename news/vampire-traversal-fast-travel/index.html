Planeshift, Shadowstep, Clawride, and Fast Travel Confirmed

The reveal clarifies how Coen crosses rooftops and moves around the wider map.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

Night abilities include Shadowstep, Clawride, and Planeshift for supernatural traversal. The developers also confirm map-based fast travel and state that Coen does not use a horse.

Why This Update Matters

Vertical vampire routes can create different approaches to buildings such as Svartrau Cathedral, while fast travel reduces repeated cross-map journeys.
