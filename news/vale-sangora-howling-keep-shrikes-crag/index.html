Vale Sangora Lore Reveals Howling Keep and Shrike’s Crag

The official location bulletin expands the valley beyond Svartrau.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

Howling Keep is a disappearing mansion in the Tantari Woods, while Shrike’s Crag is a quarry marked by Skender Dragosti’s massacre of peasant rebels.

Why This Update Matters

Both locations show that Vale Sangora contained fear, injustice, and supernatural mystery before the public vampire takeover.
