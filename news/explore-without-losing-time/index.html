Players Can Search Vale Sangora Without Losing Narrative Time

The hourglass system separates investigation time from actions that advance the deadline.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

A burning-house activity can remain available while the player explores because the world clock advances only after designated activities.

Why This Update Matters

Walkthroughs should therefore distinguish safe exploration from confirmed time-spending decisions instead of assigning a cost to every detour.

This article covers one specific player question so it can be refreshed without changing unrelated guides. The linked category hub collects the other updates that affect the same stage of the player journey.

What Is Still Unknown

Which events can expire for other reasons and how save reloads interact with time still need testing.

Those details will remain labelled unconfirmed until Rebel Wolves, Bandai Namco, an official storefront, or verified release-build testing provides evidence.

Context for Players

The Blood of Dawnwalker launches September 3, 2026 for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. It follows Coen through Vale Sangora, where meaningful actions spend a limited 30-day resource while ordinary exploration does not continuously advance the clock.
