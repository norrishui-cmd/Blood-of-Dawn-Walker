Human by Day and Vampire by Night Shapes Two Play Styles

Coen’s form changes his magic, movement, combat tools, and potential quest solutions.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

Human Coen uses swordplay and rune-based hexes, while vampire Coen gains claws, bites, supernatural movement, and instinctive powers.

Why This Update Matters

Players can lean toward one form, but the developers warn that ignoring the other means missing a large portion of the game.

What Is Still Unknown

The final rules for voluntarily changing time and the cost of waiting have not been fully documented.

Those details will remain labelled unconfirmed until Rebel Wolves, Bandai Namco, an official storefront, or verified release-build testing provides evidence.

Context for Players

The Blood of Dawnwalker launches September 3, 2026 for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. It follows Coen through Vale Sangora, where meaningful actions spend a limited 30-day resource while ordinary exploration does not continuously advance the clock.
