Dawnwalker Goes Gold Ahead of September Launch

Rebel Wolves has completed the launch build, strengthening confidence in the September 3 release date.

Official-source updateChecked July 19, 2026

What Was Confirmed

The gold milestone means the launch candidate can proceed through certification and physical production. It does not rule out a day-one update or later performance fixes.

Why This Update Matters

Players can plan around September 3, but should still wait for official preload and regional unlock schedules before booking an exact start time.

Context for Players

The Blood of Dawnwalker launches September 3, 2026 for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. It follows Coen through Vale Sangora, where meaningful actions spend a limited 30-day resource while ordinary exploration does not continuously advance the clock.
